About Me

I'm Tatsuya Yokokura. I moved to Vancouver in September 2025 to pursue web development. I'm currently studying at Cornerstone College, learning modern web technologies.

In Japan, I worked as an engineer from 2020. Before tech, I explored different industries—sales at a travel agency, and barista and manager at a café. These taught me to understand business needs from multiple perspectives.

My goal is to become an engineer who understands business context and create solutions that align with real-world needs. I'm excited to contribute to Vancouver's tech community.
